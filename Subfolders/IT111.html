Hello, Welcome

This page contains all of the outputs, lessons and learning from the lessons discussed
on the subject IT111 or the Introduction to Computing 1

LESSON 1: WHAT IS COMPUTING?

This lesson contains the following: what is computing, difference of computer and computing, history of computers, effects of computing on society and various industries. We learned here the different inventions and major things invented in different eras that the new computer has. The picture on the left is from the activity on history of computers.

LESSON 2: PROFESSION AND CAREERS IN COMPUTING

This lesson contains the following: The different professions we can pursue in the field of technology. There is numerous profession one’s person can pursue in the field of technology. It can in the programming & software development field, information systems operation & management field, telecommunications & networking field, web and internet field, computer science research field, graphics & multimedia field or in the Technical & support field. In this lesson we did some essay type of activity asking what profession we will choose.

LESSON 3-4: INFORMATION CYCLE | COMPUTER SYSTEMS

This lesson contains the following: the processing cycle, difference between data and information, phases of the cycle to process information, characteristics of good information, and components in a computer system. In this lesson’s activity, we did identification quizzes.

LESSON 5: CONCEPT OF COMPUTER SECURITY

This lesson contains the following: classifying information, common types of cybercrime, types of cybercriminals, and countermeasure to prevent cyber-attacks. In this lesson we did a case analysis regarding a famous cybercriminal case here in the Philippines and I choose the first woman who got arrested for a cybercrime case in the Philippines.

LESSON 6: HTML AND CSS

This lesson contains the following: the basics of HTML or Hyper Text Markup Language and CSS or Cascading Style Sheet. In this lesson, we made an online portfolio and the output is the website itself.
